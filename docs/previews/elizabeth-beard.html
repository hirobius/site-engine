Common questions · buyers

What buyers ask me first.

How much do I really need for a down payment?

Less than most people think — many buyers put down far less than 20%. We'll connect you with a lender to look at your real options before you worry about it.

How long does buying take in Spokane?

From accepted offer to keys is usually 30–45 days once you're pre-approved. The search itself moves at your pace and the market's.

Do I pay you as a buyer?

In most cases the seller covers the buyer-agent commission. Either way I'll be clear about any costs up front — no surprises.

What if I'm relocating from out of state?

I do this all the time. We can tour by video, and I'll be your eyes on the ground for neighborhoods, inspections, and everything in between.

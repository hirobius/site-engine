## Free change window — 7 days

For **7 days after the launch date**, we'll correct any errors and make small
content tweaks (hours, phone number, service list, a swapped photo, typos) **at
no charge**. Send everything in one message if you can — it's faster for both of us.

## After the 7-day window

After the free window closes, changes are billed:

- **Content update** (text, hours, photos, prices): _{{CHANGE_FEE}}_ per request.
- **New section or feature** (anything beyond the existing layout/config):
  quoted separately as a custom change — it's a different scope than a content edit.

## Keeping your info current is your responsibility

Once the site is live, the **accuracy of the information on it is yours to
maintain**. Outdated hours, prices, or phone numbers are a common cause of lost
customers and bad reviews — if something changes on your end, let us know within
the free window, or send a paid update request afterward. We can't be responsible
for business details we were never told changed.

## Spam protection

Your contact form is protected by a hidden honeypot and (when enabled) hCaptcha.
If you ever start getting spam through the form, tell us — turning on / tuning
captcha is a quick fix.
